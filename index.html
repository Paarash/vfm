TDH:

Pressure head immediately above the pump at wellhead

\[ TDH = h_{static \; column} + h_{friction \; loss} + h_{back \; pressure} \]

Parameters affecting TDH:

\( q_o , \; ID_{tubing} \) & fluid properties

Workflow of ESP design

Step 1: IPR \( \rightarrow \; \; q_{Ld} \; \rightarrow \) pump size (manufacturer's specification) \( q_{Lp} \ge q_{Ld} \)

Step 2: IPR \( \rightarrow \; p_{wf} \; \) for \( q_{Lp} \)

Step 3: Estimate minimum pump depth based on minimum intake pressure of the pump \[ D_{pump, min} = D - \frac{p_{wf} - p_{in, min}}{0.433 \gamma_L} \]

Cont'd

Step 4: Select the maximum possible depth of pump \( D_{pump} \ge D_{pump,min} \)

Step 5: Calculate \( p_{out} \) from TPR curve of the well

Step 6: calculate required pump head \( \Delta p = p_{out} - p_{in} \)

Step 7: Refer to pump characteristic curve \( \rightarrow \) head per stage \( \rightarrow \) number of stages

Step 8: \( total \; power = power \; per \; stage \times number \; of \; stages \)
